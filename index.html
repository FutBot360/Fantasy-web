How is the score calculated?

Players earn different amounts of points depending on the actions they perform.

  • Goals ‚Üí Each goal earns 5 points for strikers, 6 points for midfielders, and 7 points for defenders.
  • Assists ‚Üí Each assist earns 3 points for strikers, 4 points for midfielders, and 5 points for defenders.
  • Tackles ‚Üí Players earn 2 points for every 2 successful tackles.
  • Successful Passes ‚Üí Players earn 2 points for every 50 successful passes.
  • Big Chances Created ‚Üí Players earn 2 points for every 2 big chances created.
  • Shots on Target ‚Üí Players earn 2 points for every 2 shots on target.
  • Penalty Won ‚Üí If a selected player wins a penalty, you earn 1 point.
  • Clean Sheet ‚Üí Defenders earn 2 points if their team does not concede a goal.
  • Starting Eleven ‚Üí Players in the starting XI begin the match with 2 points.
  • Best in Position ‚Üí The best player in each position earns 1 additional point.
